We Live to dance

The term Kathak is derived from the Vedic Sanskrit word Katha which means "story",
and Kathakar which means "the one who tells a story", or "to do with stories".
Wandering Kathakars communicated stories from the great epics and
ancient mythology through dance, songs and music.
