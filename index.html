Courses we offer

Kathak in 3 Gharana
LUKNOW , BANARAS AND JAIPUR

Jaipur Gharana

This Gharana was intoduced by Bhanuji.It is belived that jaipur gharana comes from the traditions of Rajasthan! Generally the dances performed in this gharana starts off from the Ganesha vandana, Shiva vandana or Durga stuthi.

Lucknow Gharana

The founder of Lucknow gharana is known to be Shri Ishwari Prasad ji.It is known to originate in Allahabad.In this gharana dancers perform small todas and tukdas!

Banaras Gharana

This gharana is also known as Janaki Prasad Gharana.This gharana was originated in Varnasi.This mainly consists of pure dance, saatvik bhav and tatkaar. Intricate footwork is carried out in this form!
